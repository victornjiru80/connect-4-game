Connect 4 Rules

  • Objective: Connect four of your own circles in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • Gameplay: Players take turns dropping one circle at a time into any column.
  • Winning: The first player to connect four circles in a row wins the game.
  • Draw: The game is a draw if the board fills up completely without any player connecting four circles.
